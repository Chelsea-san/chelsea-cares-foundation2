About Us

Who We Are

Chelsea Cares Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing emotional support, advocacy, and resources to women living in the aftermath of domestic violence. Our mission is to empower and support women survivors of violence by providing them with the resources, guidance, and advocacy they need to rebuild their lives.

Mission & Vision

Mission

Our mission at Chelsea Cares Foundation is to empower and support women survivors of domestic violence by providing them with the resources, guidance, and advocacy they need to rebuild their lives. We are committed to creating a safe and nurturing environment where every woman can access the help she deserves, free from judgment or fear.

Vision

We envision a world where no woman has to suffer in silence. A world where every woman, regardless of her circumstances, has the opportunity to live a life free from abuse and fear. Through our dedicated efforts and community-driven initiatives, we strive to break the cycle of domestic violence against women and create lasting change, one life at a time.

Our programs & Services

Chelsea Cares Foundation is committed to offering vital support to women impacted by domestic violence. Our comprehensive programs are designed to provide safety, healing, and empowerment to survivors.

Emergency Shelter and Safe Housing:

We offer immediate and confidential shelter for women and children escaping abusive situations.

Counseling and Mental Health Support:

We focus on building resilience and supporting long-term recovery.

Legal Assistance and Advocacy:

We guide survivors through the legal system, offering support in areas such as restraining orders, custody cases, and other legal matters related to domestic violence.

Child Support Services:

We understand the impact of domestic violence on children.

Our Impact

Chelsea Cares Foundation has made a global impact by empowering women affected by domestic violence in over 30 countries. We have supported more than 100,000 women in escaping abuse and rebuilding their lives. Our safe housing program has provided emergency shelter to over 10,000 women, offering a secure space for recovery. Through our counseling and mental health services, over 20,000 survivors have healed from trauma and regained control of their lives. Our legal advocacy has helped countless women navigate complex legal challenges, securing protection orders and other crucial outcomes, while also contributing to policy changes that enhance protections for women globally. Chelsea Cares Foundation remains committed to creating lasting change, ensuring that women worldwide can live free from abuse and fear.

  • Equality and inclusion

  • Self-sufficiency and self-care
