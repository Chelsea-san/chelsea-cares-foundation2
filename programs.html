What We Offer

At Chelsea Cares Foundation, Our services are designed to empower, support, and help women rebuild their lives with dignity and hope.

Emergency Shelter and Safe Housing

We provide immediate and secure shelter for individuals and families in crisis. Our safe housing options offer a refuge where you can feel protected and supported, giving you the space to heal and regain your strength.

Counseling and Mental Health Support

Our team of professional counselors is here to support your mental well-being. We offer personalized counseling sessions to help you navigate your emotions and experiences, fostering mental health and resilience.

1 in 3 women globally have experienced physical or sexual violence, often by an intimate partner. Raising awareness and offering support is crucial in breaking this cycle. Domestic abuse survivors are 4 times more likely to suffer from severe mental health conditions. Our counseling services are here to help rebuild emotional well-being. Only 40% of women who experience violence seek help of any sort. Legal assistance can empower survivors to reclaim their lives and secure justice. Studies show that immediate crisis intervention can reduce the risk of further harm by 60%. Our 24/7 helpline is here to offer that crucial support."

Legal Assistance and Advocacy

Navigating the legal system can be overwhelming, but you don't have to do it alone. Our legal assistance and advocacy services ensure that your rights are protected, providing guidance and representation when you need it most.

Helpline and Crisis Intervention

If you're in a crisis, our helpline is here for you 24/7. We offer immediate intervention and support, connecting you with the resources and assistance you need to stay safe and secure.

Child Support Services

We understand the unique needs of children in challenging situations. Our child support services provide care, guidance, and resources to ensure that children have the best possible chance for a stable and healthy future.
