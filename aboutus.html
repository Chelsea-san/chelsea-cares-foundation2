Who We Are

Chelsea Cares Foundation was founded by a group of passionate advocates for women’s rights with a mission to support and empower women survivors of domestic violence.

Our Mission

At Chelsea Cares Foundation, Our mission is to provide comprehensive support to women survivors of domestic abuse. We are dedicated to offering emergency shelter, counseling, legal assistance, and advocacy to those in need. Our aim is to empower women to regain their strength, rebuild their lives, and find their voice once again.

Our Vision

We envision a world where domestic abuse is a distant memory, where every woman lives free from fear and has the resources to pursue her dreams. Chelsea Cares Foundation is more than an organization; it’s a movement toward justice, equality, and empowerment. Join us in our mission to transform lives and make a lasting impact. Together, we can create a world where every woman is empowered to rise above her circumstances and achieve her fullest potential.

Our Impact

Chelsea Cares Foundation has made a remarkable impact across over 30 countries, transforming the lives of more than 100,000 women. Our emergency shelters have provided critical safety and support to over 10,000 women in need. Additionally, our dedicated efforts have facilitated healing and recovery for over 20,000 survivors of trauma. Each achievement underscores our commitment to fostering resilience and driving meaningful change, creating a brighter future for women everywhere.

Our Values

At Chelsea Cares Foundation, our values guide every action and decision, reflecting our unwavering commitment to supporting women survivors of domestic abuse and promoting empowerment. Our values are the cornerstone of our mission and it drive us to make a meaningful difference in the lives of women around the world. Through our dedication to these principles, we strive to foster a world where every woman is empowered, respected, and able to thrive.

Why It Matters

Every statistic represents a real person with a story. Behind every case of domestic abuse is a woman who deserves safety, respect, and the opportunity to thrive. By founding this organization, Chelsea has committed to making a tangible difference in the lives of thousands of women, ensuring that they receive the care and support they rightfully deserve.

Our Programs & Services

Chelsea Cares Foundation is committed to offering vital support to women impacted by domestic violence. Our comprehensive programs are designed to provide safety, healing, and empowerment to survivors.

  • Emergency Shelter and Safe Housing:

    • We offer immediate and confidential shelter for women and children escaping abusive situations.

  • Counseling and Mental Health Support:

    • We focus on building resilience and supporting long-term recovery.

  • Legal Assistance and Advocacy:

    • We guide survivors through the legal system, offering support in areas such as restraining orders, custody cases, and other legal matters related to domestic violence.

  • Child Support Services:

    • We understand the impact of domestic violence on children.

Why We Founded Chelsea Cares Foundation

In a world where many women face unimaginable challenges, the inception of Chelsea Cares Foundation is rooted in a profound commitment to stand against domestic abuse and champion women’s empowerment. Our founder, Grasswell Chelsea Gabriella, was driven by a personal experience and a deep-seated belief in the power of compassion and advocacy.
Our Journey
Chelsea’s journey began with witnessing the harsh realities faced by women living in the shadows of domestic violence. She saw firsthand how systemic barriers and societal stigmas often left survivors feeling isolated and voiceless. Motivated by a vision of a world where every woman has the support and resources to rebuild her life, Chelsea established the foundation to create a beacon of hope and resilience.
